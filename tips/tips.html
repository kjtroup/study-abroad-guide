Travel Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks for traveling with friends or alone!

Transportation

  • Flights: Budget traveling here is extremely cheap if you have flexible dates and locations. Be prepared to pack light or pay extra for your bag. Skyscanner found me the best deals on flights.
  • Trains: I used Omio for almost all my trains except for those in Italy. I found the best deals and most options on this site.
  • Buses: If you are willing to brave a bus trip, I recommend using Flixbus. It has a good reputation and cheap options for traveling.

Stays

  • Hostels: I stayed in a lot of hostels with all-female dorms. I got to meet other women traveling and often still maintain privacy. It is not for everyone, but there are great options on Hostel World and trustworthy ratings.
  • Hotels: I rarely dabbled in hotels because of price. When I did, I used Expedia or Booking.com but avoided them for the most part.
  • Airbnbs: Airbnb has amazing options when you are traveling with a group of friends or even solo. There are so many options and different price points.

Safety

  • Keep valuables close to you
  • Be aware of your surroundings
  • Make good decisions
